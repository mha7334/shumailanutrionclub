About Me

My name is shumaila. I am studying Health and Nutrition from VIA University Aarhus. I am professional Nutritionist and certified exercise instructor. As your Nutritionist, I will help you through the process with a dietary plan we will jointly agree on. I will support you along the way and I have the knowledge needed to help you reach your desired goals.

About Shumaila Nutrition Club

Shumaila Nutrition Club is a fun and rewarding approach for eating the best for your body. As a client with me, you will get professional and individual nutritional counselling, that will be based your lifestyle, your habits and your personal goals. As your nutritionists, I will be with you every step of the way to support, motivate and guide you to success.


Structure of Nutritional Counselling Sessions:


The first interview
  • Identify and analyze your eating habits
  • Clients complete food history
  • Measurement of body fat
  • Weighing, waist circumference and BMI calculation
  • Measurement of basal metabolic rate
Your personal diet plan
  • Preparation of food intended diet plan according to current recommendations
  • Diet plan is made on the basis of the General reccomendations and the Danish dietary guidlines
  • Diet plan personalized for you
  • Diet plan generated from the first conversation
  • Relevant material and recipes
Follow Conversation
  • Measurement of weight, waist circumference, BMI, body mass index, basal metabolic rate
  • Adjustment of diet plan as needed
  • Status of compliance with the diet principles
  • you will get advice and ideas on how to continue with healthy eating habits
